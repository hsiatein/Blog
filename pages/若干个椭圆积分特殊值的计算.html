若干个椭圆积分特殊值的计算

本文章将介绍部分椭圆积分特殊值的计算方法. 椭圆积分的特殊值满足如下的等式: $$ \frac{K\prime\left( k_n \right)}{K\left( k_n \right)}=\frac{K\left( \sqrt{1-{k_n}^2} \right)}{K\left( k_n \right)}=\sqrt{n} $$

这里我们不关心椭圆积分特殊值时的对应特征模(也就是取得特殊值时, 椭圆积分的自变量)怎么求, 而是直接给出 $$ k_1=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}, k_2=\sqrt{2}-1, k_3=\frac{\sqrt{6}-\sqrt{2}}{4}, k_4=\frac{1-k_1\prime}{1+k_1\prime}=3-2\sqrt{2}... $$

可以发现上面第四个特殊值与第一个有关, 这一个关系由兰登变换来描述, 借助兰登变换可以用第\(n\)个特殊值求出第\(n4^m(m=1,2,3...)\)个特殊值

首先计算第一个特殊值, 这个可以通过简单的换元得到 \begin{align} &K\left( \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \right) =\int_0^1{\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-x^2}\sqrt{1-\frac{1}{2}x^2}}dx} \\ &=\int_0^1{\frac{1}{2\sqrt{t}\sqrt{1-t}\sqrt{1-\frac{1}{2}t}}dt}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\int_0^1{\frac{1}{\sqrt{t}\sqrt{1-t}\sqrt{2-t}}dt} \\ &=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\int_0^1{\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-u}\sqrt{u}\sqrt{1+u}}du}=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\int_0^1{\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-u^2}\sqrt{u}}du} \\ &=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\int_0^1{\frac{1}{\sqrt{1-t}t^{\frac{3}{4}}}dt}=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}}\frac{\Gamma \left( \frac{1}{4} \right) \Gamma \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)}{\Gamma \left( \frac{3}{4} \right)} \\ &=\frac{\Gamma ^2\left( \frac{1}{4} \right)}{4\sqrt{\pi}} \end{align}

然后计算第二个特殊值, 这个可以通过超几何两个最经典的变换(pfaff变换和二次变换)得到 \begin{align} &K\left( \sqrt{2}-1 \right) =\frac{\pi}{2}\,\,_2F_1\left( \frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2};1;3-2\sqrt{2} \right) \\ &\text{pfaff变换} \\ &=\frac{\pi}{2}\left( 2\sqrt{2}-2 \right) ^{-\frac{1}{2}}\,\,_2F_1\left( \frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2};1;\frac{3-2\sqrt{2}}{2-2\sqrt{2}} \right) =\frac{\pi}{2}\left( 2\sqrt{2}-1 \right) ^{-\frac{1}{2}}\,\,_2F_1\left( \frac{1}{2},\frac{1}{2};1;\frac{1-\sqrt{2}}{2} \right) \\ &\text{二次变换} \\ &=\frac{\pi}{2}\left( 2\sqrt{2}-2 \right) ^{-\frac{1}{2}}\,\,_2F_1\left( \frac{1}{4},\frac{1}{4};1;-1 \right) \\ &\text{pfaff变换} \\ &=\frac{\pi}{2}\left( 2\sqrt{2}-1 \right) ^{-\frac{1}{2}}2^{-\frac{1}{4}}\,\,_2F_1\left( \frac{1}{4},\frac{3}{4};1;\frac{1}{2} \right) \\ &\text{二次变换} \\ &=\frac{\pi}{2}\left( 2\sqrt{2}-2 \right) ^{-\frac{1}{2}}2^{-\frac{1}{4}}\,\,_2F_1\left( \frac{1}{8},\frac{3}{8};1;1 \right) \\ &=\frac{\pi}{2}\left( 2\sqrt{2}-2 \right) ^{-\frac{1}{2}}2^{-\frac{1}{4}}\frac{\Gamma \left( 1 \right) \Gamma \left( \frac{1}{2} \right)}{\Gamma \left( \frac{7}{8} \right) \Gamma \left( \frac{5}{8} \right)}=\frac{\left( \sqrt{2}+1 \right) ^{\frac{1}{2}}\Gamma \left( \frac{1}{8} \right) \Gamma \left( \frac{3}{8} \right)}{2^{\frac{13}{4}}\sqrt{\pi}} \end{align}

再次是计算第三个特殊值, 可以依靠拉马努金主定理来进行计算 \begin{align} &\int_0^{\infty}{\frac{dt}{2\sqrt{t}\sqrt{t^2-2t\cos \theta +1}}} \\ &=\frac{1}{2}\int_0^{\infty}{t^{-\frac{1}{2}}\left( \sum_{n=0}^{\infty}{\left( -1 \right) ^nP_n\left( -\cos \theta \right) t^n} \right) dt} \\ &=\frac{\pi}{2}P_{-\frac{1}{2}}\left( -\cos \theta \right) =K\left( \cos \frac{\theta}{2} \right) \\ &=K\left( \cos \frac{\theta}{2} \right) \\ &\theta =\frac{5\pi}{6}\Longrightarrow \int_0^{\infty}{\frac{dt}{2\sqrt{t}\sqrt{t^2+\sqrt{3}t+1}}}=K\left( k_3 \right) \\ &=\sqrt[4]{3}\int_0^{\infty}{\frac{du}{2\sqrt{u}\sqrt{u^2+3u+3}}}=\sqrt[4]{3}\int_0^{\infty}{\frac{du}{2\sqrt{u}\sqrt{\left( u+\frac{3}{2} \right) ^2+\frac{3}{4}}}} \\ &=\sqrt[4]{3}\int_1^{\infty}{\frac{du}{2\sqrt{u-1}\sqrt{\left( u+\frac{1}{2} \right) ^2+\frac{3}{4}}}}=\sqrt[4]{3}\int_1^{\infty}{\frac{du}{2\sqrt{u^3-1}}} \\ &K\left( k_3 \right) =\frac{3^{\frac{1}{4}}\Gamma \left( \frac{1}{3} \right) ^3}{2^{\frac{7}{3}}\pi} \end{align} 拉马努金主定理的证明可以参考梅林变换应用于贝塞尔函数的第一章

最后是第四个特殊值的计算, 先给出之前提到的兰登变换公式 $$ \begin{cases} \frac{K\prime\left( \frac{1-k\prime}{1+k\prime} \right)}{K\left( \frac{1-k\prime}{1+k\prime} \right)}=2\frac{K\prime\left( k \right)}{K\left( k \right)}\\ K\left( \frac{1-k\prime}{1+k\prime} \right) =\frac{1+k\prime}{2}K\left( k \right)\\ \end{cases} $$ 使用此公式可以很容易地算出 \begin{align} &k=k_1=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}\text{时},k\prime=\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \\ &\frac{K\prime\left( \frac{1-k\prime}{1+k\prime} \right)}{K\left( \frac{1-k\prime}{1+k\prime} \right)}=\frac{K\prime\left( 3-2\sqrt{2} \right)}{K\left( 3-2\sqrt{2} \right)}=2\frac{K\prime\left( k_1 \right)}{K\left( k_1 \right)}=2 \\ &K\left( k_4 \right) =K\left( 3-2\sqrt{2} \right) =\frac{\left( \sqrt{2}+1 \right) \Gamma ^2\left( \frac{1}{4} \right)}{8\sqrt{2\pi}} \end{align}
