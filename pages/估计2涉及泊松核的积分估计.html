估计2涉及泊松核的积分估计

这次所带来的估计是两个涉及到泊松核的积分估计

第一个是泊松核一次方 $$ \begin{align} &{\color[RGB]{240, 0, 0} f\left( x \right) =a_0+a_1x+a_2x^2+o\left( x^2 \right) } \\ &{\color[RGB]{240, 0, 0} \int_0^{\pi}{\frac{f\left( x \right)}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx}\sim \frac{\pi a_0}{2}\frac{1}{1-t}-a_1\ln \left( 1-t \right) +o\left( \ln \left( 1-t \right) \right) } \end{align} $$

第二个是泊松核二次方 $$ \begin{align} &{\color[RGB]{240, 0, 0} f\left( x \right) =a_0+a_1x+a_2x^2+a_3x^3+a_4x^4+o\left( x^4 \right) } \\ &{\color[RGB]{240, 0, 0} \int_0^{\pi}{\frac{f\left( x \right)}{\left( 1-2t\cos x+t^2 \right) ^2}dx}} \\ &{\color[RGB]{240, 0, 0} =\frac{\pi a_0}{4}\frac{1}{\left( 1-t \right) ^3}+\left( \frac{\pi a_0}{8}+\frac{a_1}{2} \right) \frac{1}{\left( 1-t \right) ^2}+\left( \frac{\pi a_0}{8}+\frac{a_1}{2}+\frac{\pi a_2}{4} \right) \frac{1}{\left( 1-t \right)}-\left( a_3+\frac{a_1}{6} \right) \ln \left( 1-t \right) +o\left( \ln \left( 1-t \right) \right) } \end{align} $$

证明1: $$ \begin{align} &\left( 1 \right) \int_0^{\pi}{\frac{1}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx} \\ &=\int_0^{\pi}{\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{t^{n-1}\frac{\sin nx}{\sin x}}dx} \\ &=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{t^{n-1}\int_0^{\pi}{\frac{\sin nx}{\sin x}dx}} \\ &=\pi \sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{t^{2n-2}}=\pi \frac{1}{1-t^2} \\ &\left( 2 \right) \int_0^{\pi}{\frac{\sin x}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx} \\ &=\int_0^{\pi}{\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{t^{n-1}}\sin nxdx} \\ &=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{t^{n-1}}\int_0^{\pi}{\sin nxdx} \\ &=\sum_{n=1}^{\infty}{\frac{2t^{2n-2}}{2n-1}}=\frac{1}{t}\ln \left( \frac{1+t}{1-t} \right) \\ &\left( 3 \right) \int_0^{\pi}{\frac{a_2x^2+o\left( x^2 \right)}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx}=O\left( 1 \right) \\ \\ &f\left( x \right) =a_0+a_1x+a_2x^2+o\left( x^2 \right) =a_0+a_1\sin x+a_2x^2+o\left( x^2 \right) \\ &\int_0^{\pi}{\frac{f\left( x \right)}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx} \\ &=\int_0^{\pi}{\frac{a_0+a_1\sin x+a_2x^2+o\left( x^2 \right)}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx} \\ &=a_0\int_0^{\pi}{\frac{1}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx}+a_1\int_0^{\pi}{\frac{\sin x}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx}+\int_0^{\pi}{\frac{ax^2+o\left( x^2 \right)}{1-2t\cos x+t^2}dx} \\ &=\pi a_0\frac{1}{1-t^2}+\frac{a_1}{t}\ln \left( \frac{1+t}{1-t} \right) +O\left( 1 \right) \\ &=\frac{\pi a_0}{2}\frac{1}{1-t}-a_1\ln \left( 1-t \right) +O\left( 1 \right) \end{align} $$
